Privacy Policy for CartTotalIQ

Effective date: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Christian App Empire LLC ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the CartTotalIQ mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: ZIP code, preferred store name, and cart item details you enter in the App.
  • Purchase information: purchase status and entitlement data for in-app purchases (for example, ad removal). We do not receive your full payment card details.

  • Provide core app features (cart calculations, tax-related estimates based on ZIP code, and saved preferences).

  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot crashes, prevent abuse, and improve the App.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide services, maintain legitimate business records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. Retention periods may vary by data type.
