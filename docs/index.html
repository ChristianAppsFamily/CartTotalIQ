Privacy Policy

CartTotalIQ - Shopping Price Scanner

Christian App Empire LLC is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information. We may collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Shopping Data: Cart items, prices, store names (stored locally on your device)
  • ZIP Code: Used only to calculate local sales tax (stored locally)
  • Advertising ID: For serving personalized ads (with consent)

Camera and Photos

CartTotalIQ uses your camera to scan price tags. Photos are processed in real-time using AI vision and are not stored on our servers. All image processing happens securely.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect data to serve personalized ads.

On iOS, we request App Tracking Transparency permission before accessing your advertising identifier. You can choose to receive non-personalized ads or purchase "Remove Ads" to eliminate all advertisements.

In-App Purchases

We offer a "Remove Ads" in-app purchase. Transactions are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store payment information.

Data Storage

All shopping data, including cart items, prices, and settings, is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
